WHAT DO I DO?

Right now, I’m a 3rd semester Multimedia Design student at Copenhagen Business Academy. I’m deeply passionate about UX/UI design — how everything connects, and how theory can be transformed into practical, user-friendly solutions. I thrive on problem solving, and no challenge feels too big when it comes to learning and improving my skills. What excites me the most is how UX/UI overlaps with graphic design and branding. Together they create not just functional interfaces, but meaningful experiences that communicate identity and values. For me, design is more than aesthetics — it’s about storytelling, creating value, and finding that balance between creativity and clarity.
